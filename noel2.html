For humanity’s project 2 we had to take a piece of inspiration from the industrial revolution and write a poem about it. Then after we had to make a collage inspired by our inspiration and our poem. Soon after we had to write an artist statement explaining our reasons for making the poem and the collage and our principal of composition. For the second portion of humanity’s project 2 we had to pick a subject that relates to social media and write a poem and make a collage relating to our subject. Soon after we finished all of those we had to write an artist statement to explain our reasons for choosing to place certain things in our project picked to display.
